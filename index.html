About Darwin Labs

Blockchain and Smart Contracts will add efficiency in many industries like healthcare, insurance, banking, trade finance etc in next 5 years. At BlockSmiths, we are building solutions which can fundamentally change some key processes of these industries.

We are building a new blockchain which is going to be specifically focussed at developers. And, we are trying to get every one to try their hands at becoming a blockchain developer. In sometime, we would be having great APIs and SDKs for developers to build their DApps based on Karachain. Blockchain is a new technology and not many investors understand it. We are running an incubator for Blockchain Startups. The startups we will invest in will be able to use the knowledge base of our team and resources.
